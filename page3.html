LastPass

LastPass is a helpful tool for keeping online accounts and sensitive info safe. It's like a locked digital box for storing strong passwords and secret stuff.

It protects against:

  • Password Theft: LastPass makes strong, unique passwords for each account, making it hard for threat actors to get in.
  • Phishing Attacks: It fills in login details only on real websites, so fake sites can't trick it to steal info.
  • Data Breaches: LastPass looks out for any hacks that might affect email or accounts and sends alerts quickly for protection.
  • Dark Web Check: It keeps an eye on the hidden dark web for signs of stolen data and sends alerts if anything suspicious is found.
